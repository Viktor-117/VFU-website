    Support

VFU is a volunteer organization supporting Ukraine with professional expertise.

We connect verified volunteers with financial sponsors and provide them with the necessary logistical support to reach the areas where they are most needed.

Our efforts are coordinated with partners, officials, NGOs, and local volunteers on the ground.

NGOs support

We support and enable smaller volunteer groups and projects on the ground in Ukraine to have a meaningful impact in their communities.
